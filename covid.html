UV-C Lights

UV-C lights are a useful piece of disinfecting equipment that is used at Surreel Cleaning Solutions Inc as a protective measure against COVID-19. By using ultraviolet radiation, UV-C lights work to kill off unwanted microorganisms on work surfaces in an eco-friendly manner. They do so through penetrating a microorganism’s cells and damaging its nucleic acids to make it no longer active. Since the COVID-19 virus is a single positive-strand RNA virus, it does not have a complete cell structure, making it easy to kill with UV-C lights. These lights can be used on surfaces, as well as in water and air purification, to eliminate unwanted and potentially harmful microorganisms.

We offer a few variations of UV-C lights in our cleaning services as effective solutions for targeting COVID-19. Our ultraviolet germicidal lamp is a 360 degree, multidirectional sanitation lamp that operates using spectral wavelengths of 454 nanometers. These lamps can immediately kill viruses, bacteria, and even mould! With a sterilization rate of at least 99%, this lamp is incredibly safe when used around animals and humans, as its micro-wave sensor will shut off the lamp automatically if it detects movement. This feature is incredibly important when using UV-C lights, as they can cause harm to humans and animals with frequent direct contact.

Another UV-C light that Surreel Cleaning Solutions Inc uses to tackle COVID-19 are portable UV-C wands. These wands work great in hard to reach places, as they can disinfect areas anywhere up to 3cm to 6cm away and only take 30 seconds to kill surface bacteria! Their portable design allows for excellent user flexibility, ensuring that you can reach different places and disinfect them with safety and ease. By using light wavelengths of between 270-282 nanometers, the UV-C portable wands effectively destroy the molecular structure of DNA and RNA viruses.

UV-C Bags are another addition to Surreel Cleaning Solutions Inc line of COVID-19 cleaning equipment. These bags come equipped with built-in gravity sensors and work by tilting to turn on their ultraviolet light automatically. Should you need to disinfect electrical equipment, for example, then the UV-C Bag is an excellent option for quick disinfecting of specific equipment and is incredibly safe to use as no light is exposed during the disinfection process.

Their ability to react with microorganisms and almost every organic chemical in the air allows Hydroxyl Generators to effectively clean the air we breathe by oxidizing and decomposing volatile organic compounds (VOCs). By using Hydroxyl Generators continuously in our work environments, we can ensure high levels of deep sanitation and constant elimination of harmful microorganisms, including COVID-19.
